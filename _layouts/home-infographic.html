Welcome

Suggestions on how to use this resource:

1. Browse! You can search the collection in a random order, release year, and historical date.

2. Do you have a specific interest in an event, topic, or time? Use the search feature to search for films that mention a keyword in its description.

For example, search terms include, “Korean War”, “1980s”, “Family”, and “IMF”.

If you’re still unsure of where to start, conduct a brief web search for recommended films by other watchers, film critiques, and scholars.
